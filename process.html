FAQ
How fast can you get here? ▼
Most on-site jobs in the Westchase core (77042/77077) are same-day. I'll give you an honest ETA when you reach out — usually within 1–3 hours during business hours.
What if you can't fix it? ▼
You don't pay for labor — period. The only charge that stands is a $35 diagnostic fee if I've spent time actively investigating. That fee gets credited toward the repair if you choose to proceed with a follow-up solution.
Do I need to buy my own parts? ▼
For repairs, I'll tell you exactly what parts to order and where to get them at the best price. For PC builds, you buy everything — I provide a custom parts list and labor only. This keeps my costs (and your costs) lower with zero markup.
Where do we meet for on-site visits? ▼
Wherever is convenient for you — your home, apartment, office lobby, or public parking area. For first-time visits I'm happy to meet in a public spot like a coffee shop or library parking lot.
Do you offer ongoing support for businesses? ▼
Yes — the Small Business Remote Plan is $79/month for two priority remote sessions plus a dedicated response line. Perfect for small offices in the Westchase area that need occasional tech help without a full-time IT person.
