Common Questions

FAQ

How long does a typical project take? +
It depends on the scope and complexity. Simple IT support tasks can be resolved in hours. Consulting engagements typically run 2–4 weeks. Larger engineering or research projects may span 1–3 months. We always provide a clear timeline in our proposal.
Do you work with individuals or only businesses? +
Both. We serve B2B and B2C clients. Whether you're a solo entrepreneur, small business, or established company — we tailor our approach to your specific situation and scale accordingly.
How do payments work? +
We accept one-time and recurring payments securely through Stripe. For projects, we typically require a deposit upfront and collect the balance at delivery. For retainer services, we offer monthly subscription billing. All payments are processed through our secure Pay page.
Are you available for ongoing support? +
Absolutely. Many of our clients engage us on a monthly retainer basis for ongoing IT support, digital marketing management, or consulting. We offer flexible plans that can be adjusted as your needs evolve.
What if I'm not satisfied with the work? +
Your satisfaction is our priority. We include revision rounds in every project and work with you until you're happy with the outcome. If there's ever a concern, we address it directly and promptly — transparent communication is a core part of how we operate.
