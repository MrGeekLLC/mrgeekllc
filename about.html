// The Story

Built on Real Skills.
Not a Storefront.

Mr. Geek LLC started with a simple observation: most tech shops make you come to them. You drop off your device, wait a day or two, and pay whatever the bill says. There's a better way.

We're a mobile-first operation based in Houston's Westchase corridor. No retail overhead means lower prices and faster turnaround — because the work happens where you are, not where it's convenient for us.

Every service is flat-rate and quoted upfront. Parts are sourced by you at cost — no markups. If we can't fix it, you don't pay for labor. That's the deal, always.
