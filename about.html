Our Story

Built on Expertise.
Driven by Results.

Mr. Geek LLC was founded with a simple mission: bring enterprise-level technical expertise to businesses and individuals who need it most. We believe that access to quality professional, scientific, and technical services shouldn't be limited by company size or budget.

Operating as a lean, remote-first firm, we combine deep multi-disciplinary expertise with the agility to respond quickly to your needs. Whether you're a startup needing your first IT setup or an established business tackling a complex engineering challenge, we bring the same precision and dedication to every engagement.

As a Georgia-registered LLC, we're proud of our roots while serving clients coast to coast. Our remote capabilities mean geography is never a barrier to getting world-class professional services.
