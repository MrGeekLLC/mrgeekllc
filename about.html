About Mr. Geek_

A Houston-based mobile tech service built on real skills, honest pricing, and showing up — literally — for every client in the Westchase area.

Built on Real Skills.
Not a Storefront.

Mr. Geek LLC started with a simple observation: most tech shops make you come to them. You drop off your device, wait a day or two, and pay whatever they say the bill is. There's a better way.

We're a mobile-first operation based in Houston's Westchase corridor. No retail space overhead means lower prices for you and faster turnaround — because the work happens where you are, not where it's convenient for us.

Every service is flat-rate and quoted upfront. Parts are sourced by the client at cost — no markups, no inflated part prices hiding in the bill. And if we can't fix it, you don't pay for labor. That's the deal.
