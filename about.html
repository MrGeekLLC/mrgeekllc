// Who We Are

Local Tech Help.
Done Honestly._

Mr. Geek LLC is a one-person, mobile-first tech service based in Houston's Westchase area. No storefront, no markups, no runaround — just the work.

Built for
Westchase.

Mr. Geek LLC was built on one observation: most people don't want to drive their laptop to a strip mall and wait two days to get it back. They want someone to show up, fix it, and be honest about what it cost.

That's the whole model. Mobile. Flat-rate. No markup on parts. I come to your home, your office, or wherever is easiest — and the price is agreed before I touch anything.

The background behind the service: computer repair, custom PC builds, device setup, and troubleshooting — plus years of logistics, customer-facing work, and figuring out real problems under pressure. That combination is what makes this more than just "IT help."
